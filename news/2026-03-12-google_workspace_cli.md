## What it is

Google workspace cli is a **one command-line tool for Drive, Gmail, Calendar, Sheets, Docs, Chat, Admin, and more. Includes Ai agent skills.** Wait.. did they say AI agent skills? Yes. It's not just for human. It's built for AI agent to deal with google services.

## Why it stands out

Why are we intrigued by google workspace cli?

- You know, it's GOOGLE.

Can't wait to use it😍

Btw, what is MCP and CLI?

[MCP](https://modelcontextprotocol.io/docs/getting-started/intro) is a protocol that allows **AI agent** and **resources** to communicate each other! We already know what HTTP protocol is. MCP is just a language which is used by AI and external program, not by web server and client.

This let AI agent access to DB, email ... and AI agent can read data and control the services. For instance, if you just put a prompt like "Can you send an email to Jessica to postpone my meeting in the afternoon?" to AI agent, your agent will do work for you.
